  • General Questions:
    • What are your regular hours?
    • We are open every day of the week between 6:00am and 12:00am (Last wash needs to start before 10:30pm)!
    • It's a holiday, is No Spot Laundry open?
    • We may be closed, depending on the holiday. Our Google Maps page will have accurate holiday hours, so please check before visiting if you are unsure.
    • Do you do dry cleaning?
    • We do not, at this time we only have the facilities to process traditional laundry.
    • Are you hiring?
    • We are always open to new talent! If you are friendly, hardworking, reliable, and looking for part-time work, we'd love to meet you. Tell us about yourself (including contact info and a resume) in an email to info@nospotlaundry.com and we’ll be in touch!
    • What forms of payment do you take?
    • Our machines accept coins, credit, debit, and our Frequent Customer Loyalty Cards. We have an ATM and a change machine on site as well.
    • What is a Frequent Customer Loyalty Card, and how do I get one?
    • Our loyalty card is the easiest way to save even more at No Spot Laundry! You will receive a 10% discount every time you use it. You can purchase, load and reload your reusable loyalty card anytime you like at the kiosk in our laundromat or here online! Then, just use it to pay for washers and dryers like you would any other card, and watch the savings add up.
  • Self-Service Questions:
    • Where do I park?
    • We have plenty of parking right in front of No Spot Laundry in our large lot. No driving around for 15 minutes looking for a spot or parallel parking necessary!
    • What are your “busy hours”?
    • Our busiest day of the week is always Sunday, with peak washing hours occurring between 9:00am and 9:00pm, followed by Saturdays and evenings. Our quietest times are Tuesdays through Thursdays between 6:00am and 3:00pm.
    • How long does a laundry cycle take?
    • Our commercial washers and dryers are fast! Most wash cycles take around 25-30 minutes, and dryer times are typically 30-40 minutes.
    • Do you offer laundry products for sale onsite?
    • You bet! we offer Tide, Gain, and All Free Clear detergents, Clorox and Oxiclean bleach, and Snuggle or Suavitel fabric softener.
  • Drop Off Wash & Fold Questions:
    • Are there any clothes you cannot clean with your Drop Off Wash & Fold service?
    • Garments that have glued on decorations, delicate beading, or are made out of fibers such as silk are not recommended, those are better gently hand washed. If in doubt, ask us when you drop off!
    • What is the turn around on a typical drop off order?
    • You will be able to pick up your items after 24 hours (Monday – Friday)! If you drop off on a Friday, your clothing will be ready for you the following Monday.
    • Is there a minimum charge?
    • We do have a minimum charge of $17.50 for drop off orders.
    • Will my clothes be washed or dried with other customer’s clothes?
    • Absolutely not! We wash and dry everyone's laundry order individually in separate machines.
    • Do I need to sort my clothes by different colors?
    • No need, we will do all sorting for you! We just ask that you put items with specific stains you'd like us to treat in a separate bag and let us know to treat them before washing.
    • Do I need to weigh my order?
    • We have a commercial, trade certified scale that we use to weigh all orders, much easier than a household scale!
    • Can you treat stains?
    • We can definitely pre-treat stained items before we wash them. Some are beyond our capability of removing, but we are successful in getting out many stains.
    • Please place all items you would like pre-treated in a separate bag and let us know in your instructions.
    • How will I know when my order is complete?
    • We'll send you a text to let you know that your clothes are sparkling clean and ready for pickup!
  • Pick Up & Delivery Questions:
    • Are there delivery fees?
    • There are no pickup or delivery fees at No Spot Laundry, you only pay for the weight of the laundry that you'd like us to clean!
    • Do I get to keep my laundry bag?
    • Absolutely! Let us know if you have more laundry then the bag can hold and we will send you a second one for your convenience.
    • What is the turn around on a typical Pick up & delivery order?
    • Your laundry will be delivered to your door just one business day after we pick it up.
    • Is there a minimum charge for pickup?
    • For our Pick Up & Delivery service, We have a minimum charge of $35.
    • Will you remind me to leave my laundry out for pickup?
    • Of course! You will receive a notification from us a day prior to your pick up to let you know to leave your laundry out for us.
    • What if I’m not home when you pick up or deliver?
    • No problem - just leave your laundry somewhere we can access it and let us know of its location in your Pickup/Delivery instructions. Many people have us leave their laundry at their back door or on the porch if they are not home. Just make sure it's protected from the elements!
    • I missed my pickup day. what now?
    • You can just arrange another pick up time, and we'll come back around for you! We do reserve the right to charge a $10 missed pickup fee.
    • Will I be notified when my laundry is delivered?
    • No need to wait by the window, we'll send you a text when we've delivered your clothes. It's that easy!
  • Commercial Laundry Questions:
    • Do I need to sign a contract?
    • None whatsoever, We'll have to earn your business each and every time!
    • Do you lease laundry items or linens?
    • We don't currently lease these items.
    • How do i get a quote for my business?
    • Call us at 816-313-8238 or write us at info@nospotlaundry.com. We'll give you 20% off your first order, just for trying out No Spot Laundry!
