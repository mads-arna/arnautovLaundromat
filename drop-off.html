Drop Off Wash & Fold Service

Not enough time in the day to wait around for laundry? Let us help you! It's as easy as 1,2,3!

  • 1) You Drop Off
    Simply come into our laundromat and leave your dirty laundry with one of our friendly attendants. let us know of your preferences and any other pertinent information. Then go and enjoy your day! we’ve got this handled.
  • 2) We Wash, Dry, and Fold
    We always wash and dry your clothes separately from other orders. We sort and straighten your items as well as pre-treat any stains before starting the washing process.
    We then wash and dry them as if they were our own, taking the utmost care to make sure that your valued clothing gets the highest quality cleaning. If you have items that you would prefer to have line dried, as opposed to our standard tumble drying, just let us know and we will hang them up to dry. All items are carefully folded as they are removed from the dryers or lines, resulting in a wrinkle-free, fresh look. If you'd like some or all of your clothes hung instead of folded, just bring us your hangers and we'll do that as well. We can provide hangers upon request for $.25.(each or all??)After folding, your clean laundry will be neatly bagged. When we are all finished with your clothes, we will text you and let you know that they are ready for you. Have special instructions? Just let us know! we will do our best to accomadate you.
  • 3) You Pick Up
    Your clothes will be ready for pick up either the same day or the very next business day. after we text you, just stop by and grab them from our attendents. Congratulations, you're finished with laundry!
  • We always do your laundry your way:
    • -Choose your detergent from: Tide, Gain, All Free & Clear, or OxiClean
    • -Choose to add Downy fabric softener or/and Downy Unstopables scent boosters for an extra luxurious feel
    • -Do you want a special detergent or softener/scent that we don't have? no problem! just provide it with your order, and we’ll use whatever you would like
    • -If you want bleach used, please let the attendant know and we will make it happen
    • -Have a stain on one of your items? Please let your attendants know and we will try our best to get it out
