  • Not enough time in the day to wait around for laundry? Let us help you!
    • Our Drop Off Wash & Fold Services are available Monday through Friday, from 10am to 8pm. All it takes is three easy steps!fluff and fold

    • 2) We Wash, Dry, and Fold
    • We always wash and dry your clothes separately from other orders. Our staff take care to wash and dry on medium heat. If you have items that you would prefer to have line dried, as opposed to our standard tumble drying, just let us know and we will hang them up to dry.
