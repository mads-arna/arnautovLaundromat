    • Your satisfaction is our first priority, but we still need to agree to some rules to ensure that there are no misunderstandings about No Spot Laundry's (referred to as "we," "us", or "our") or your obligations during our time together.
    • By using our services you acknowledge and accept the following Terms and Conditions:
    • laundering suitability
    • You agree that your clothes can be washed in warm water, and dried using medium heat in a tumble dryer. We cannot be liable for any damage to clothing that is not suitable for a standard laundering process. We can not examine each individual garment tag prior processing the laundry, therefore, if something is “dry-clean only,” or otherwise unsuitable for common washing and drying techniques, please do not include it in the laundry you give to us.
    • We also reserve the right to decline cleaning any piece of laundry if we think it may cause damage to our equipment or our staff. This includes heavily soiled items or items with biohazards such as urine, feces, blood, etc. as well as laundry from households with known infections such as bed bugs, etc. This is for the health and safety of our team and our customers.
    • Payment Terms and Conditions:
    • Payment for services is due at time of service. All new Wash & Fold, Pickup & Delivery and Commercial accounts are set up with credit/debit cards on file for auto-payment and payment will be collected before laundry is returned.

    • We do everything we can to avoid damaging your clothing, but there are things that even we miss. Please check your pockets prior to washing; we can not be responsible if something left in your laundry causes damage to your items. what happens if damage machines?
    • All laundering processes cause normal wear and tear on clothing. As a result, we are not responsible for any wear and tear of laundry, including: loss of buttons, fading, fatiguing, or abrasions.
    • color bleeding
    • We sort your laundry in order to provide the best service possible and we use “color catchers” to prevent bleeding of items. However, we can’t be held responsible for the re-disposition of colors (aka bleeding) or shrinking / stretching.
    • Lost Laundry
    • We have a very robust tracking system for all orders from the time they are picked up / dropped off to the time they are delivered back to you. We tag all your bags and loads with your name, and that tag travels with your laundry to ensure all items are returned to you. We also have systems in place to check each work area before and after so that no laundry is left behind.
    • However, we do not assume any responsibility for missing pieces of laundry. Because laundry is processed by the load, and not “per item”, we simply have no efficient way of verifying that we received any particular piece of laundry in the first place.
