    • laundering suitability
    • You agree that your clothes can be washed in warm water, and dried using medium heat in a tumble dryer. We cannot be liable for any damage to clothing that is not suitable for a standard laundering process. We can not examine each individual garment tag before processing the laundry. Therefore, if something is “dry-clean only,” or otherwise unsuitable for common washing and drying techniques, please do not include it in the laundry you give to us.
    • We also reserve the right to decline cleaning any piece of laundry if we think it may cause damage to our equipment or our staff. This includes excessively soiled items or items with biohazards such as urine, feces, blood, etc.. This also includes laundry from households with known infections such as bed bugs or other transmissible pests. This is for the health and safety of our team and our customers.
    • Payment
    • Payment for services is due at time of service. If you do not want our service, please let u know and we will cancel your wash. Although we truly hope you are satisfied with our services, once we have laundered your clothes, we cannot offer a refund. All new Wash & Fold, Pickup & Delivery and Commercial accounts are set up with credit/debit cards on file for auto-payment and payment will be collected before laundry is returned.
    • You can send us any amount of laundry you choose, but any loads weighing less than 10 pounds will be rounded up to our minimum weight of 10 pounds. To make life simpler, we round to the nearest pound when weighing your laundry to determine if you have met the required minimum weight (see below). Our weights are final. If you have not met the minimum weight, we will promptly notify you of any surcharge.

    • We have a very robust tracking system for all orders from the time they are picked up / dropped off to the time they are delivered back to you. You also agree that our records of pick-up and drop-off times are true and accurate.

    • You're free to cancel any appointment up to 1 hour prior to the start of your delivery window, so if you don't have any laundry for us to wash on a particular week, please be sure to notify us more than 1 hour before your appointment time so we don't come to your address and need to charge a no-show fee. We require at least 1 hour's cancelation notice for residential appointments, at least 2 hours notice for commercial appointments.
