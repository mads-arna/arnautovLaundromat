Pick Up & Delivery Service
  • Clean clothes just got even easier!
    • Are you tired of spending your precious time doing never-ending laundry? Do you find it challenging to fit laundry time into your busy schedule? Is your washer broken or not efficient? Would you just prefer to never touch a washer or dryer again?
    • That's why we're here!
    • Our pickup and delivery service allows you to enjoy clean clothes without ever leaving the comfort of your home. It's that simple!
    • Now serving: Raytown, Kansas City, Lees Summit, Greenwood, Leawood, Overland Park, Country Club Plaza, Westwood, Westwood Hills, Mission, Mission Hills, Mission Woods, Roland Park, and Prairie Village-- with more coming soon!
    • 1) Schedule your Pickup:
    • Hit the Schedule Pickup button to get started! You'll let us know the specifics of how to best treat your valuable clothing, as well as a good pickup/delivery time. You can also set up reccuring pickups whenever you'd like!
    • 2) We Collect Your Laundry:
    • On the day of pickup, collect and bag your laundry. For your first pickup, please feel free to use garbage bags or any other disposable bag (no hampers or baskets please). 13 gallon garbage bags best match the size of our laundry bags. We'll give you a No Spot laundry bag free of charge when we deliver your clean clothes! This bag is yours to keep, even if you decide to stop using our Pickup & Delivery service.
    • If you have any items that need pre-treating for stains or other special requests, please bag those separately with a note. Make sure all bags are labeled as laundry so we don't accidentally take a wrong bag. That's it! You don't need to be waiting around for pickup, just let us know where and when and we'll come grab your clothes.
    • 3) Clean Laundry is Delivered Back to You:
    • Each order is washed separately and tagged throughout the process to ensure your items never mingle with other orders. Your clothes will be washed, dried, and promptly folded and bagged. They will be returned to you the very next day, neatly packaged for both privacy and protection. We'll bring it right to your doorstep!
